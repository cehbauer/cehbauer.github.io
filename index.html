Which two websites are you most inspired by?

I am most inspired by the Helms Workshop and Anthropologie websites.

How do they exhibit effective use of space, size or balance?

Both sites have a strong emphasis on art and design. Anthropologie's site prominently features a great deal of photography combined with illustration, while Helms Workshop highlights a lot of illustration in addition to photos of their portfolio. They both utilize white space and are very balanced.

What makes them similar and different?

Many elements are similar, but you can see that the Helms Workshop site is comprised of three columns, with rotating images at the top that span the three columns. The Anthropologie site is static, while Helms Workshop has moving images that switch every few seconds. Both utilize a lot of color and visual imagery. The imagery featured on both sites is clearly the focus, with text being secondary.
